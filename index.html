Super Metroid Randomizer Tracker


The Super Metroid Randomizer Tracker is meant to serve as a resource to anyone running the Super Metroid Item Randomizer hack. This hack changes the locations of every item in the game. While playing, certain advanced item prediction strategies can be derived by looking at the locations of previously discovered items. This site gives players an area to track item locations throughout a playthrough.

This site was inspired by the Metroid Prime Randomizer Helper by Pwootage. He has trackers for Metroid Prime 1 and 2, but not one for Super Metroid. I plan to continue work on this site, implementing more features as my knowledge of web design and Super Metroid improves.
